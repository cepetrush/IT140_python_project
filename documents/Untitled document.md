**Storyboard (Description and Map)**

You are a hungry mouse in need of an afternoon snack. You are craving a gourmet, 4 cheese \- grilled cheese sandwich. A delicious blend of Swiss, Cheddar, Brie, and Gouda melted to perfection between two generously buttered pieces of fresh Italian bread. As luck would have it, multiple mouse traps have been set around the house, baited with the specialty cheeses that you require to make a gourmet, 4 cheese \- grilled cheese sandwich. You, being a mouse of considerable skill, have no trouble removing the cheese from the trap without setting it off. The challenge lies in avoiding the house cat, Mr. Whiskers, who prowls through the house guarding the traps and looking for unwelcome intruders. Your goal is to gather all the ingredients needed to make a gourmet 4 cheese \- grilled cheese sandwich: Swiss from the Study, Gouda from the Grand Ballroom, Cheddar from the Cellar, Brie from the Bedroom, Italian bread from the Pantry, and Butter from the Kitchen. All while eluding Mr. Whiskers who would love nothing more than to make you HIS afternoon snack.
